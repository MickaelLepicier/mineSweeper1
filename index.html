Welcome to LEGO MineSweeper

Objective:

    The goal is to clear all the squares on the field that do not contain mines.

How to Play:

  • Left-click on a square to reveal what's underneath.
  • - If it's a mine you loose 1 live.
  • - If it's a number, that number indicates how many mines are around that square.
