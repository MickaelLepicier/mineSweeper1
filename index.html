Welcome to LEGO MineSweeper

Start the game by clicking a cell on the board

Objective:

    The goal is to clear all the cells on the field that do not contain mines.

How to Play:

    We play with the mouse.
  • Left-click on a cell to reveal what's underneath.
    • If it's a mine you lose 1 life.
    • If it's a number, that number indicates how many mines are around that cell.
  • Right-click on a cell to place a flag where you think a mine is hidden.
  • Use the numbers as clues to deduce where the mines are located and flag them.

Winning the Game:

    When all the cells without mines have been revealed and all the mines have been flagged, except the triggered mines.

Losing the Game:

    If you click on a cell with a mine and you don't have 0 lives.
